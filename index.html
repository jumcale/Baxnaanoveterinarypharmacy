Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

At Baxnaano pharmacy and veterinary clinic, our mission is to provide comprehensive and compassionate veterinary care and pharmaceutical services to ensure the health and well-being of livestock and other animals. We strive to offer the highest quality medical care and customer service, treating every animal.

Our Vision

At Baxnaano pharmacy and veterinary Clinic, our vision is to be the leading provider of innovative and comprehensive veterinary and pharmaceutical care for livestock. We aim to enhance animal health and productivity, ensuring the well-being of livestock and the success of the farming community. By leveraging advanced medical practices, cutting-edge technology, and a deep understanding of agricultural needs, we strive to set new standards in livestock healthcare.

Our Identity

Baxnaano Pharmacy & Veterinary Clinic

Baxnaano Pharmacy and Veterinary Clinic is a professional livestock healthcare facility established on 5 September 2023 and located in Arabsiyo, Gabiley, Somaliland. The clinic is owned and led by Dr. Guleid Abdilahi Muhumed, a qualified veterinary professional dedicated to advancing animal health and supporting the livestock sector.
